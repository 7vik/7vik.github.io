About Me


I'm Satvik Golechha, a fledgling writer. I write books, screenplays, poetry, blogposts, and self-referential statements. I also want to work on films and songs. I live in India.

I am a BITS Pilani alumnus, and I'm also interested in Computer Science, AI-research, reading, lots of sports, and teaching-learning. When I'm not writing, I'm working at Wadhwani AI (actually, it's the other way round). We're a group of amazing people working on AI for Social Good. This website focuses on none of these though (for now).

I believe that education and entertainment should not be a privilege based on how much someone or their parents earn. Hence, I keep everything I write freely available online. After some time, I'll add a link for voluntary payments. Until then, you can contact me personally.

The best way to contact me is through e-mail. I am zsatvik _at- gmail _dot- com.
